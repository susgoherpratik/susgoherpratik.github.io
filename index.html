About

My Journey in this amazing industry started as a Quality assurance Tester (Game Tester) at Ubisoft. Learning and understanding my role and the exposure to multiple projects gave me an opportunity on understanding my strengths and hone my skills.

Developing a keen eye for Game design, and the importance of design on the user's experience piqued my interest in Designing. Preparing and learning to follow my interest, I joined as a level designer through IJP's (hard work lol). Working as a Level Designer helped me in gaining some unique expertise and a better understanding of game design and the creative process. I also got an opportunity to connect & collaborate with teams from studios all over the world, viz. France, China, Canada, & Romania.

I've worked on multiple Titles and gained a varied experience in different genres. I also lead a small team for a mini project successfully which taught me a lot, adding unique experiences to my repertoire.
